## What It Is

Claude Opus 4.7 is the May 2026 Opus refresh from [[anthropic]], announced at **Code w/ Claude 2026** (May 6 2026) — [[willison-code-w-claude-2026]]. The headline differentiator versus prior Opus generations is that **[[claude-design]]** (visual design capability, previously a partially-documented surface inside Claude.ai) ships as a built-in capability of Opus 4.7 itself, not as a separate harness.

## Strengths & Weaknesses

- **Visual design integration**: the most notable change — designed-by-Claude artifacts (UI mocks, brand systems, presentations) are produced natively by the model rather than by a downstream harness pipeline.
- **Standard frontier-tier reasoning**: positioned as the top of Anthropic's tiered family alongside the Mythos preview ([[claude-mythos]]); detailed benchmark deltas vs. Opus 4.6 not in the public event coverage captured.

## When to Use It

- **Visual + design-heavy work**: explicit positioning makes Opus 4.7 the default Anthropic choice for end-to-end design + code workflows previously tackled with Claude Design + Opus 4.6 in tandem.
- **Drop-in upgrade path**: announced at Code w/ Claude 2026 as a successor to Opus 4.6; expected to take over the Opus tier in Anthropic-hosted products (Claude Code, Cowork, Claude.ai).
