## What It Is
OpenAI's second-generation image generation model. Released April 21, 2026 as "ChatGPT Images 2.0." Rolling out to all ChatGPT users. Significant capability jump over previous image generation.

## Strengths & Weaknesses
**Strengths**: 
- Strong text rendering — readable embedded text in images
- Complex grid layouts: previously 3x3 struggled; now near-perfect at 10x10
- 360 equirectangular image generation
- Photorealistic output — community reaction: "can't believe it's 100% AI"

## When to Use It
Complex image generation tasks requiring accurate text, grid layouts, or photorealistic editorial illustration. Strong for information visualization prompts.
