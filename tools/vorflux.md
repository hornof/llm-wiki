## What It Is

**Vorflux** (@vorfluxai) is a cloud **"autopilot for software engineering"** launched 2026-07-14 by **Prasanna S** (@myprasanna, ex-cofounder/CTO of Rippling, self-described "#1 coder in India"). Its pitch is a hard line between **autopilot and copilot**: *"Every AI coding tool still makes you fly the plane. That's the copilot model: you stay in the seat, approving every turn. The models quietly got good enough to fly the whole route, but the tools never caught up. So we built the autopilot."* Runs in the cloud (not your laptop) so agents aren't laptop-bound.

> ⚠️ **Signal caveat.** Sole source is the founder's **launch-day self-promotional thread** ([[vorflux-autopilot-launch-2026-07-14]]) with a $200-free-credits offer. No independent usage or benchmark evidence captured. Founder pedigree (Rippling) and a **$15M YC-led seed** are the hard signals; capability claims are self-asserted.

## Key Concepts

- **Autopilot vs copilot** — the core framing: fly the whole route autonomously vs stay-in-the-seat-approving-every-turn (the [[claude-code|Claude Code]] / [[codex|Codex]] copilot register).
- **"The Great Flattening"** — Prasanna's thesis that *"code stopped being the hard part this year,"* so planning/design/review/execution collapse toward the system that runs the work: *"One person inside, or zero."* A firm-shape restatement of the [[ai-native-organizations|AI-native-organization]] thesis.
- **The harness (model-neutral)** — *"That judgment needs somewhere to live. It lives in the harness: the one place your engineering principles run on every session, lossless and compounding. We train no models, so it stays neutral. The labs ship their opinion. Nobody ships yours."* A vendor-side statement of the [[loop-engineering|harness-primacy]] thesis — the harness as the durable, model-agnostic layer that carries *your* judgment.
