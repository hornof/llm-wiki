## What It Is

Chinese AI lab (branded **Z.ai**, formerly/also **Zhipu AI**) that develops the **GLM** family of large language models, released as **open weights**. As of mid-2026 its [[glm-5-2|GLM 5.2]] is the first open-weights model widely described as a genuine competitor to closed frontier models like Opus and GPT.

## Why It Matters

Z.ai is the current face of the **open-weights commoditization** pressure on frontier labs: by shipping near-Opus quality at ~1/5 the price with open weights (trivial switching cost), it anchors the [[ai-margin-collapse|AI margin collapse]] thesis. Strategically relevant to how durable any US frontier lab's inference margin actually is, and to the geopolitics of open-weights models given the [[claude-fable-5|export-control]] backdrop.

## Products

- **GLM family** — open-weights LLMs. Latest tracked: [[glm-5-2|GLM 5.2]] (~$4.40/MTok via providers like Fireworks; no vision, weaker web search).
