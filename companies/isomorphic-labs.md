## What It Is
A Google DeepMind spin-out focused on AI-driven drug discovery. Led by Demis Hassabis alongside his Google DeepMind CEO role. Builds on [[alphafold]] protein structure predictions to design the drug compounds that bind to those structures.

## Problem It Solves
AlphaFold tells you the 3D shape of a protein and what's on its surface. Isomorphic Labs builds the next step: AI systems that design chemical compounds that bind strongly to the target site on the protein but not to anything else (which would cause toxic side effects). This covers "adjacent technologies in more biochemistry and chemistry space." — [[hassabis-deepmind-alphafold-agi]]

## The Vision
- Run 99% of drug discovery exploration **in silico** (computationally)
- Reserve wet lab steps for validation only
- Reduce drug discovery timelines from ~10 years to months, then weeks or days
- Enable personalized medicine — personalized variations of base medicines

"I think if we can do that — and I think we can get there in the next few years — I think we could reduce drug discovery times instead of taking like an average of 10 years down to months, maybe even weeks, and perhaps even days one day." — Hassabis, [[hassabis-deepmind-alphafold-agi]]
