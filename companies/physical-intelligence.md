## What It Is

Physical Intelligence (pi) is a San Francisco AI startup (founded 2024) training foundational models for robots. It collects training data from human teleoperators who control humanoid robots in realistic environments — kitchens, bedrooms — to teach models physical world interactions. Raised $1B. Listed on Forbes 2026 AI 50.

## Technical Approach

Trains foundation models for robotics using human teleoperator data collected in realistic physical environments (homes, kitchens, etc.). The goal is models that generalize across physical tasks — the robotics equivalent of what GPT-4 did for language tasks.
