## What It Is

Kyndryl (NYSE: KD) is an IT infrastructure services company, **spun out from IBM in November 2021** as the largest corporate spinoff in the technology sector at that time. Approximately ~80,000 employees worldwide; revenue ~$15B annually. Core business: managing the IT infrastructure (mainframes, hybrid cloud, network, security operations) of large enterprises. **First wiki capture via [[kris-lovejoy|Kris Lovejoy]]'s 2026-05-15 *Eye on A.I.* interview** — [[eye-on-ai-lovejoy-agentic-enterprise-2026-05-15]].
