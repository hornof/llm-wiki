## What It Is

Y Combinator (YC) — early-stage startup accelerator, founded 2005. Twice-yearly batches; deals are standardized as a SAFE for ~$500K. Best-known portfolio includes Airbnb, Stripe, Dropbox, Coinbase, OpenAI (early funding), DoorDash, Reddit, Instacart, Twitch.

For this wiki, YC's primary relevance is its **Requests for Startups** (RFS) — semi-annual lists of ideas YC wants to see founders tackle. The RFS is treated as a high-signal demand-side leading indicator: when a YC partner publishes an RFS item, it usually means deal flow has not caught up with what the partner thinks should exist.

## Traction Signals

YC traction in *this wiki* is treated as a meta-signal — when YC names a category, it typically validates a thesis the wiki has been tracking from another angle.

- **April 2026 — YC Summer 2026 RFS**: 15-item list with two items overlapping the [[company-brain]] thesis (Tom Blomfield: "Company Brain"; Diana Hu: "The AI Operating System for Companies"). Direct YC primary-source validation of [[sentra]]'s positioning. — [[yc-summer-2026-rfs]]
- **AI-Native Service Companies category** (Gustaf Alströmer): YC partner-level validation of [[saas-disruption-thesis]] (Naval) and the broader [[ai-native-organizations]] move from copilots → outcome delivery.
- **Software for Agents category** (Aaron Epstein): YC partner-level validation of [[mcp]] and the agent-native infrastructure trajectory; "while everyone else is building agents, the biggest opportunity might be building the software those agents depend on."
- **Inference Chips for Agent Workflows** (Diana Hu): YC explicitly names the chip-architecture-for-agent-loop gap that adjacent practitioners (e.g. [[chamath-decision-context-agents]]) have been flagging from the engineering side.
- **AI Personalized Medicine RFS** (Ankit Gupta): explicitly names [[claude-code]] as "an agent harness" for analyzing personalized health data — Anthropic-product call-out at YC partner level.

## Caveats

- YC RFS is *aspirational deal flow*. Not every RFS item produces a generational company; many produce nothing. Treat as a "what some smart partners think is missing" signal, not as a prediction.
- This page focuses on AI-era YC and the RFS-as-signal pattern; the broader YC history is well-covered elsewhere.
