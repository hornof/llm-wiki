## What It Is

Reflection is a Brooklyn-based AI startup (founded 2024) building open-source AI models explicitly positioned as an American alternative to Chinese open-source models like DeepSeek. Has raised $2.1B and is valued at $8B as of the Forbes 2026 AI 50 list.

## Background

Founded by Misha Laskin and Ioannis Antonoglou — both former Google DeepMind researchers who led early efforts on the Gemini model family. The company has not publicly released its own AI as of April 2026, but is building a data center in South Korea and developing bespoke AI systems for Korean companies.

## Investors

Backed by Nvidia, Sequoia, Lightspeed, and 1789 Capital (where Donald Trump Jr. is a partner) — an unusual combination that reflects the geopolitical framing of the venture.

## Geopolitical Angle

Reflection's founding rationale is explicitly tied to the Trump administration's interest in promoting an American alternative to Chinese open-source AI. The combination of government-adjacent investors (1789 Capital) and a South Korea data center signals a dual US-Asia strategy.
