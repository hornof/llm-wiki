## What It Is

Sequoia Capital is a prominent venture capital firm with offices in the US, India, China, and Southeast Asia. It has backed many significant technology companies over its history. In AI, Sequoia has been active both as an investor and as a convener — hosting events like AI Ascent to bring together founders, researchers, and operators in the AI space.

## AI Ascent

AI Ascent is Sequoia's AI-focused conference event. The 2026 edition featured speakers including Greg Brockman, Andrej Karpathy, Demis Hassabis, Boris Cherny, and Dmitri Dolgov, with 150+ leading founders in attendance. Sessions are published as a YouTube playlist. — [[ai-ascent-2026]]
