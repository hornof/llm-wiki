## What It Is

Cursor is an AI coding software company (founded 2022, San Francisco) that builds an AI-powered code editor used by software developers. Valued at $29.3B as of April 2026. Has raised $3.3B. Listed on Forbes 2026 AI 50.

## Competitive Position

Cursor sits in a market now also contested by Anthropic's Claude Code and OpenAI's Codex. The Forbes AI 50 article explicitly frames Cursor as a player that "must innovate to compete" as the frontier AI labs move into the coding agent market. This is a significant signal: being on the AI 50 list at a $29.3B valuation while facing direct competition from the labs whose models you depend on.
