## What It Is

**Poolside AI** (Eiso Kant) is a coding-focused AI lab pursuing a **"model factory"** strategy — a small team betting that ruthless focus on the *training infrastructure, data, and evals* beats throwing raw scale at the problem. Surfaced via the Latent Space *"Inside the Model Factory"* episode ([[dailybrief-roundup-2026-07-23]]). *(Single-source; primary not fetched.)*

## Traction Signals

- **2026-07 — trained a 118B-MoE model reportedly beating [[thinking-machines|Thinky's]] (Thinking Machines') open weights** ([[inkling]]): a small-team result at a frontier-ish tier. The thesis: *"the bottleneck isn't GPUs — it's taste."* Their model (referenced as **Laguna**) is the test of whether model-factory discipline can out-execute mega-lab scale. *(Claims per the Latent Space episode; independent benchmarks not captured.)*
