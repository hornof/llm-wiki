## What It Is

Thinking Machines Lab is an AI research and product company founded in 2025 by Mira Murati, former CTO of OpenAI. Headquartered in San Francisco. Has raised $2B. Listed on Forbes 2026 AI 50 as one of four female-led companies on the list.

## Key People

- **Mira Murati** — founder and CEO; former OpenAI CTO; one of the most prominent AI leaders to leave a frontier lab and found a new venture
