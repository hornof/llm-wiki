## What It Is

AI research lab founded by David Silver, who spent 13 years at Google DeepMind. Headquartered in London. Mission: "To make first contact with superintelligence." Building what Silver calls a "superlearner" — an AI that discovers all knowledge from its own experience, without pre-training on human data or internet scraping.

## Key People

- **David Silver** — founder; former Google DeepMind researcher; principal architect of AlphaGo, AlphaZero, and AlphaStar

## Funding

- **$1.1B seed round** — the largest seed round in European history as of April 2026
- **$5.1B pre-money valuation** at seed stage
- **Investors**: Sequoia Capital (led the deal; flew to London personally), Nvidia ($250M+), Google, British Government (Sovereign AI fund)

## Technical Thesis

Silver argues that current frontier LLMs — ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Grok — have a fundamental ceiling: they can remix human knowledge but cannot discover genuinely new knowledge because they are trained on human-generated data. His alternative is the same approach that produced AlphaGo, AlphaZero, and AlphaStar: reinforcement learning from self-play, starting with only the rules of a domain, no human demonstrations. The company compares its ambition to Darwin: "Where Darwin's law explained all Life, our law will explain and build all Intelligence."

This approach is distinct from both:
- **LeCun / [[ami-labs]]**: world models / JEPA (also rejects LLM scaling, but via architecture)
- **Hassabis / [[google-deepmind]]**: continual learning + creativity (working within the LLM + RL paradigm)
