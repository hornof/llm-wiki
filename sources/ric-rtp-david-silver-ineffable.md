## Summary

Long-form Twitter thread by @Ric_RTP summarizing David Silver's departure from Google DeepMind and the founding of Ineffable Intelligence. Silver raised $1.1B — the largest seed round in European history — at a $5.1B valuation, backed by Sequoia, Nvidia, Google, and the British government's Sovereign AI fund. The thread argues Silver's bet is that the entire LLM-centric industry is building toward a hard ceiling, and that RL-from-self-play (the approach behind AlphaGo, AlphaZero, AlphaStar) is the right path to superintelligence.

## Key Claims / Takeaways

- David Silver spent 13 years at Google DeepMind, leading AlphaGo, AlphaZero, and AlphaStar — all learned via self-play RL without human training data
- Silver left DeepMind and founded Ineffable Intelligence in London
- Raised $1.1B seed round at $5.1B valuation — largest seed round in European history as of April 2026
- Investors: Sequoia Capital (led, flew to London personally), Nvidia ($250M+), Google, British Government Sovereign AI fund
- Core thesis: LLMs trained on human data can remix but not discover — they have a ceiling; RL from self-play has no such ceiling
- "Move 37" in AlphaGo vs. Lee Sedol (1 in 10,000 probability of any human playing it) cited as evidence that self-play RL can exceed human knowledge frontier
- Mission: "To make first contact with superintelligence"
- Company analogy: "Where Darwin's law explained all Life, our law will explain and build all Intelligence"
- This is the third $1B+ raise in ~2 months by a senior AI researcher founding a lab with zero products: LeCun/AMI Labs ($1B), Murati/Thinking Machines Lab ($2B), Silver/Ineffable ($1.1B)
