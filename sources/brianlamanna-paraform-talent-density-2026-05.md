## Key Claims / Takeaways

- **Three AI labs in top 3** — Thinking Machines Lab (Murati), OpenAI, Anthropic. [[thinking-machines-lab]] in #1 position despite being product-less and pre-revenue is striking; talent-density ranks the founders' ability to recruit, not the company's revenue or product surface.
- **Cursor at #4** — strong rebuttal to the [[saas-disruption-thesis]] reading that frontier-lab competition (Claude Code + Codex) is breaking Cursor. Talent density holding above $30B+ companies suggests Cursor is still pulling top engineers.
- **Vertical AI surfacing on the list** — Harvey (legal), Abridge (medical), Decagon (customer support), Glean (search), Hebbia (research), Rogo (financial research) — supports the [[linas-anthropic-startup-playbook-2026-05]] vertical-AI thesis. Five of the top 25 are vertical-AI companies; this is now a recognized category for talent.
- **AI infrastructure cluster** — Modal Labs, Together AI, Fireworks AI, Baseten, Anyscale, Pinecone — six dedicated AI infra companies in the top 35. The "model-agnostic infra layer" continues to attract top engineers despite frontier-lab consolidation.
- **Coding-specific cluster** — Cursor, Cognition (Devin), Warp, Augment, Factory, Traversal — six coding-tool companies in the top 35. Coding agents remain an open competitive surface even with [[claude-code]] as a dominant CLI.
- **Inside the LaManna comment thread**: Ramp called out as the "wildly impressive" non-AI mention; LaManna said it was the only company he took a call from in 5 years at Gong.

## What's Notable by Absence

- **No Google DeepMind, Microsoft AI, Meta, or Apple** — talent-density rankings appear to filter for venture-stage / standalone companies. Frontier-lab researchers at Big Tech are ranked elsewhere or excluded by methodology.
- **No [[google-deepmind]], [[ami-labs]], [[ineffable-intelligence]], [[safe-superintelligence]], [[reflection]]** — the "neo-labs" cluster from [[ai-50-brink-list-2026]] is absent, suggesting Paraform's methodology may require some minimum org/staff size threshold.
- **No [[block]], no [[luma-ai]], no [[world-labs]], no [[physical-intelligence]], no [[isomorphic-labs]]** — implies the index leans toward companies actively recruiting through Paraform's network rather than a market-wide talent map.

## Provenance Caveat
