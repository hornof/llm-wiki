### Run-rate vs ARR (the load-bearing methodological caveat)

The hot-take framing flags the key terminology distinction:

- **ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)** = revenue locked in via contracts that recur over a year, smoothed for cancellation patterns.
- **Run-rate revenue** = the latest month or quarter multiplied to annual scale. Captures recent-velocity but not contract-stickiness.
- Anthropic publishing *run-rate* rather than *ARR* signals what the brief framing calls *"adoption velocity to investors, not profitability."*

**Implication for tracking**: until Anthropic publishes ARR-vs-run-rate breakdowns, the wiki should treat $47B as *upper-bound monthly-velocity* rather than *contract-locked annual revenue.* The churn-cost-of-the-velocity isn't captured.
