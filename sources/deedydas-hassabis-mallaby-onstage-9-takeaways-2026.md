## Summary

Deedy (@deedydas, Menlo Ventures) summarizes an on-stage conversation between Demis Hassabis and Sebastian Mallaby in SF as "the 9 best things they said." Quotes below as reported by Deedy (secondhand; no official transcript linked).

## Key quotes

**Hassabis:**

1. **Career advice (the keeper):** *"Immerse yourself in AI tools. Everyone has access to tools 3–6 months behind frontier. Enormous opportunity lies in applying AI to unexplored areas."*
2. On lab leaders: *"Dario is the best of all the other lab leaders."*
3. On India: *"Not all countries are pessimistic about AI. I was just in India for the AI Summit Modi had and they're quite optimistic there."*
4. On Isomorphic: *"We need 6 'AlphaFold' moments to compress the drug delivery timeline from 10yrs to a few months."*
5. On p(doom): *"I don't think of p(doom) as probabilities to throw out there. I just know it's non zero. Some people like Marc Andreesen and Yann LeCun think it's 0% and I think that's crazy."*
6. On AGI: post-scarcity world, "unbelievable amount of science," equitable-sharing economics, *"need great new philosophers."*
7. On the future: pictured a CERN-like collaborative endgame; *"The decisions we make in the next 5-10 years are going to affect us for 1000s of years. But I remain very optimistic."*

**Mallaby:**

8. *"There is a 50% chance that OpenAI goes bankrupt in the next 18mos."*
9. On Claude Mythos: *"It's not really tenable for a private company to decide who gets access to the frontier of cyber defense tech. What happens when China can do this in 6-12mos?"*

## Notes

- The career-advice line is the on-stage, sharper sibling of Hassabis's Hard Fork "immerse yourself now / ninja" advice (May 2025) — this version adds the "3–6 months behind frontier" access claim and the "unexplored areas" opportunity framing.
- Quotes are Deedy's paraphrases/transcriptions, not verified transcript.
