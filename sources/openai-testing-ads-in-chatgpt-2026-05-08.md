## Key Claims / Takeaways (from secondary synopsis)

- **What's launching**: ad placements inside ChatGPT, rolling out to **free-tier users**.
- **Privacy framing**: OpenAI states ads have "clear labeling" and "privacy controls" — wording consistent with the OpenAI brand framing of consent and user control.
- **OpenAI's stated rationale**: sustains free-tier access at scale.
- **Open question** (per 2026-05-09 neutral brief): "How will ad presence affect reasoning quality, or will it remain cosmetic?" — the practitioner concern is whether ads modulate or contaminate model behavior, not just sit alongside it.
