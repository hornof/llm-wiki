## Summary

**Alex Imas** + **Phil Trammell** on the Dwarkesh Patel podcast (2026-06-04) — **first wiki-captured load-bearing post-AGI economics framing** organized around the *"what stays scarce when intelligence is no longer scarce?"* question. Reframes post-AGI economics away from labor scarcity toward **capital + coordination + presence-bound goods**. Pairs structurally with [[altman-three-observations-2025-02|Altman's Three Observations]] super-exponential-value framing at the cost-collapse layer; with [[anthropic-institute-when-ai-builds-itself-2026-06-04|the same-day Anthropic Institute RSI publication]] at the capability-acceleration layer; and with [[ai-roi-gap|the AI ROI-gap thesis]] at the *capability-distribution-vs-existence* layer.

## Key Claims / Takeaways

### The scarcity-shift framing

> *"Post-AGI, the scarce thing won't be intelligence—it'll be the stuff that doesn't scale. Ballerinas, surgeons, coaches. Anything requiring presence."* (Brief hot-take framing)

> *"The real constraint post-AGI isn't computation—it's human attention and presence... scarcity doesn't vanish, it shifts from 'can we do this' to 'who gets access to limited humans.'"* (Brief insightful framing)

**Pattern**: scarcity is conserved across the AGI transition; it shifts from *intelligence-as-scarce-input* to *presence-as-scarce-output* and *coordination-as-scarce-glue*. The economic-question shifts from *"can we do this?"* (capability problem) to *"who gets access to limited humans?"* (allocation problem) — **a political-economy problem, not a tech one**.

### Pairs with Altman's super-exponential socioeconomic-value claim

Cuts orthogonally to [[altman-three-observations-2025-02|Altman's Observation 3]] (*"socioeconomic value of linearly-increasing intelligence is super-exponential"*):

- **Altman**: total economic output scales super-exponentially with intelligence (assumes intelligence is the binding constraint and value is the dependent variable).
- **Imas + Trammell**: intelligence stops being the binding constraint; presence + coordination + capital + access take over.

**First wiki-captured load-bearing counter-framing to Altman's super-exponential thesis from a post-AGI economics-side perspective**. **Both can be true**: total output scales super-exponentially *and* most of the new value is captured by holders of the presence/coordination/capital-side complements.

### Pairs with the Anthropic Institute RSI publication

Same-day publication ([[anthropic-institute-when-ai-builds-itself-2026-06-04]]) supplies the *capability-acceleration evidence*; Imas + Trammell supply the *post-acceleration economic-organization framework*. The Institute publication frames RSI as the *technical possibility*; Imas + Trammell frame the *economic-organization-shift if RSI succeeds*.

### Brief amplification of "presence-bound" examples

Three named examples surface in the brief: **ballerinas, surgeons, coaches**. Pattern: domains where the *delivered service is the human-presence itself*, not the cognitive work the human performs. Distinct from *"jobs that require thought"* (which AGI eats); these are *jobs that require physically-being-the-thing*.

**Pattern-watch**: which categories of "presence-bound" goods become operationally measurable scarcity-anchors? Likely candidates: live performance, in-person care, status-as-attention (Imas's research domain), peer-attention-based-trust networks.

### Cross-cutting framings

- **Pairs with [[ai-labor-market-impacts|Timeline-Calibration Hexangle]] (now 7 corners after Anthropic Institute 2026-06-04 update)**: Imas + Trammell don't supply a *timeline* anchor; they supply an *economic-organization* anchor that becomes load-bearing whenever the technical timeline closes. **Should be tracked as a structural-economic-framework anchor alongside the timeline anchors.**
- **Pairs with [[ai-roi-gap|ROI-gap thesis]] at the *who-captures-the-value* layer**: the gap thesis says vendors capture but operators don't; Imas + Trammell extend this to the post-AGI period where *neither* vendor nor operator captures the value if the binding constraint shifts to presence-bound humans. **First wiki-captured "post-AGI capture-extraction" framing** — value created may flow to a 3rd category (presence-holders) rather than vendor or operator.
- **Pairs with [[deedydas-sf-ai-wealth-divide-2026-05-15|Deedy SF wealth-divide]] and [[dailybrief-roundup-2026-06-01|Sanders sovereign-wealth-fund proposal]]**: if presence-bound humans capture the post-AGI value, the *distribution mechanism* for the rest of society becomes the load-bearing political question. Sanders's sovereign-wealth-fund framing operationalizes this as a public-equity-mechanism; Imas + Trammell frame the underlying scarcity-shift the mechanism would need to address.
- **Pairs with [[saas-disruption-thesis|SaaS-disruption thesis]] at the *what-stays-defensible* layer**: Naval's *"pure software is uninvestable"* framing was about software-replaceability by AI; Imas + Trammell extend the question to *what remains valuable post-AGI*. Software isn't on the list; presence-bound services are.
- **Pairs with [[andrewng-ai-fde-vs-ai-engineer-2026-06-01|Ng's 5 future-specialist-role predictions]]**: Ng's roles (LLMOps, Evals, AI Data, Harness, FDE) are all *AI-adjacent technical roles*; Imas + Trammell's framework predicts that *presence-bound non-technical roles* become structurally more valuable. **Bracketing the future-jobs question from opposite ends** — Ng on the AI-engineering side, Imas + Trammell on the non-AI-engineering side.
