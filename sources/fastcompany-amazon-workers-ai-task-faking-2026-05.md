## Key Claims / Takeaways

- **Mandate-driven AI usage** at Amazon produces fake-task generation. Workers are gaming an AI-utilization metric rather than adopting AI into their actual workflow.
- **Goodhart's Law instance**: the moment AI-utilization-rate becomes the measured target, it stops being a meaningful proxy for AI-driven productivity. Workers route their existing work *around* the metric by inventing AI-touched busywork to register adoption.
