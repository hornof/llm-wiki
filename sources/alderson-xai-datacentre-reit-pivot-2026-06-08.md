## Key Claims / Takeaways

### The thesis

- **xAI is pivoting to a datacenter-rental business model**
- Frame: the lab is now structurally a **REIT** (real-estate investment trust) — landlord economics, not research economics
- Implication: *"frontier labs don't fund themselves; REITs do"*

### Luke-side hot-take framing

> *"xAI pivoting to a datacenter-rental model is the honest move. Frontier labs don't fund themselves; REITs do. Elon's just saying the quiet part out loud."*

### Luke-side insightful framing

> *"The REIT framing exposes a real structural shift: if your moat is compute access, not model superiority, you're building landlord economics, not lab economics. The question isn't whether xAI does this — it's whether anyone building frontier models can avoid it."*

**First wiki-captured "frontier-lab-as-landlord" structural framing**.
