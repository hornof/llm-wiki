## Summary

[[charlie-hills|Charlie Hills]] (@charliejhills, charliehills.substack.com) posts a viral thread + infographic, **"Build Your Whole Team with Claude,"** presenting **42 curated [[skill-md|Claude skills]] laid out as a real company org chart** — CEO node `claude-code` ("the operating system") over seven departments (Developers, Designers, Marketing, Social Media, Finance, Small Business, Legal). Every tile is a **real, installable** skill/plugin, sourced from GitHub repos and `claude.com/plugins/*`. The load-bearing claim, echoed by a top reply, is *"every skill is actually installable, not concept art."* It's a concrete datapoint on the **maturing Claude skills/plugins distribution ecosystem** and the **"company-as-a-stack-of-skills"** framing.

## Key Claims / Takeaways

- **Org-chart-of-skills framing**: `claude-code` sits at the top as "The Operating System" / CEO; the seven departments each hold a column of named skills with cute role titles (e.g. Developers → *Skill Forge, Docs Fetcher, Tool Wright, Skill Smith, QA Engineer, Memory Keeper*). The metaphor makes an abstract skills library legible as a company you "staff."
- **Everything is installable, from mixed sources** — a distribution snapshot:
  - **First-party**: `github.com/anthropics/skills` (Skill Creator, MCP Builder, Webapp Testing, Web Artifacts, Brand Guidelines) and Anthropic's **`claude.com/plugins/{finance,small-business,legal}`** hosted plugin bundles.
  - **Third-party OSS**: `obra/superpowers` (14-skill pack), `upstash/context7` (live library docs), `thedotmack/claude-mem` (persistent memory), `Leonxlnx/taste`, `Jakubantalik/*` (transitions), `nextlevelbuild*` (UI UX Pro Max), plus creator repos (`coreyhaines31/*` marketing, `charlie947/*` social).
- **The "42" is the curated chart, not the total**: the bundled department plugins claim far more than their six visible tiles — Marketing "45 skills," Small Business "31," Social Media "17," Legal "9," Finance "8." So the real installable surface is ~100+ skills; 42 is the legible org-chart selection.
- **Skeptic counter-signal in replies** (worth keeping): *"42 skills but you're still the one making every actual decision… congrats on being a solo founder with extra steps"* (@SlopToSignal) and *"Revenue = 0"* (@opcastil) — the same [[zerqfer-ghost-economy-ai-personas-2026-07-08|"ghost economy"]] / [[ai-roi-gap|ROI-gap]] skepticism that shadows the AI-native-solo-founder narrative. Chad Justice's reply ("I run mine in Discord with war rooms where multiple agents discuss things") is a live datapoint on how practitioners actually orchestrate these.
