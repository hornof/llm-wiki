## Summary
TechCrunch exclusive (March 2026) on Luma's launch of Uni-1, the first model in their Unified Intelligence family, and Luma Agents. Covers architecture, capabilities, and Jain's "intelligence in pixels" framing.

## Key Claims / Takeaways
- **Uni-1 architecture**: decoder-only autoregressive transformer; text and images in a single interleaved sequence; trained on audio, video, image, language, and spatial reasoning
- **"Intelligence in pixels"**: model "thinks in language and imagines and renders in pixels" — single reasoning framework for all modalities
- **Luma Agents**: powered by Uni-1; handle creative workflows end-to-end; persistent context across assets; self-critique and iterative refinement
- **Benchmark performance**: outscores Google and OpenAI image models on reasoning benchmarks at 30% lower cost (March 2026)
- **Architect analogy**: like an architect who holds "an internal mental representation of the structure, light, spatial dynamics, and lived experience" — unified reasoning not narrow task optimization
- **Roadmap**: audio and video output in subsequent Unified Intelligence releases
