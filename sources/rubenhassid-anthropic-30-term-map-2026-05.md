## Summary

Practitioner glossary by [@rubenhassid](https://x.com/rubenhassid) listing 30 terms across 10 categories that map the Anthropic / Claude product surface as of May 2026. Promotional framing ("Anthropic ships faster than anyone can keep up") with an infographic; substance is a synoptic snapshot of *every named surface, mode, and primitive* in the Claude ecosystem at this moment.

Most useful as a **reference index** — not a deep dive on any one item, but the most complete single-post inventory of named Claude products in the wiki to date. Several surfaces appear here that the wiki had not yet recorded: **Claude in Excel, Claude in Chrome, Computer Use, Dispatch, Memory (cross-chat), Adaptive Thinking, Scheduled Tasks**.

## The 30 Terms

### 1. The 3 Models
- **Opus** — "the smartest. Use it for thinking & writing."
- **Sonnet** — "quick edits. Scanning files. Light tasks."
- **Haiku** — "short answers. Cheap. Forget it exists." *(practitioner dismissal — note as one-person opinion, not a Wiki claim about Haiku 4.5)*

### 2. The Apps You Already Know
- **Chat** — the basic text box on claude.ai
- **Cowork** — "Chat + folders. This is where you live." → [[anthropic]] (Cowork named publicly 2026-05-05 as a plugin host)
- **Claude Code** — "for devs. Skip it unless you build." → [[claude-code]]

### 3. The Apps You Don't Know Yet
- **Claude Design** — "builds websites. No code." → [[claude-design]]
- **Claude in Excel** — "lives in your spreadsheet." *(first appearance in wiki)*
- **Claude in Chrome** — "a browsing agent." *(first appearance in wiki — distinct from [[playwright-mcp]] which is the Microsoft MCP browser-automation server)*

### 4. Workspaces & Memory
- **Projects** — "one workspace per task."
- **Custom Instructions** — prompt scoped to Projects
- **Memory** — "Claude remembers across chats." *(first appearance in wiki — cross-chat consumer-side memory feature, distinct from [[mem0]] and from [[company-brain]])*

### 5. Outputs
- **Artifacts** — docs, code, apps in a side pane
- **Markdown** — `.md` files. "Claude's favorite format."
- **CLAUDE.md** — "the memory file Claude Code reads"

### 6. The Skills System
- **Skills** — "saved prompts. Trigger with / command."
- **SKILL.md** — the trigger & instructions inside skills *(see [[writing-claude-code-skills]])*
- **Plugins** — "bundles of Skills + Connectors for a job."

### 7. Connections
- **Connectors** — links between Claude & your apps
- **Computer Use** — "It clicks & types on your screen." *(first appearance in wiki — Anthropic's screen-control capability)*
- **Dispatch** — "phone app to fire tasks to the desktop." *(first appearance in wiki)*

### 8. Power Modes
- **Adaptive Thinking** — "makes it think before output." *(first appearance in wiki — toggleable extended-thinking mode)*
- **Research** — "deep web research for a full report."
- **Web Search** — "live results from the internet."

### 9. Smart Helpers
- **AskUserQuestion** — clickable form to prompt the user
- **Scheduled Tasks** — "Claude on a recurring timer." *(first appearance in wiki — see also `loop` and `schedule` skills referenced in user environment)*
- **Global Instructions** — prompt Claude reads before tasks

### 10. The Foundations
- **Prompt** — "the text you send. Files > prompts."
- **Styles** — "saved tone presets. Don't sound like AI."
- **Vibecoding** — "building anything by just prompting." *(see [[vibe-coding]])*
