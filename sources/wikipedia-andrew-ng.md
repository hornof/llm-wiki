## Summary
Wikipedia article on Andrew Ng — co-founder of Google Brain, former Chief Scientist at Baidu, Stanford professor, co-founder of Coursera, founder of DeepLearning.AI and Landing AI. One of the world's most influential AI educators; 8 million students have taken his courses.

## Key Claims / Takeaways
- Co-founded Google Brain (2011) with Jeff Dean — foundational large-scale neural network research at Google
- Former Chief Scientist at Baidu (2014–2017); built AI platform DuerOS
- Co-founded Coursera (2012) with Daphne Koller; now 8M+ students in his courses
- Founded DeepLearning.AI — leading AI education platform (Machine Learning Specialization, Deep Learning Specialization, AI for Everyone, etc.)
- Founded Landing AI — AI-powered SaaS for enterprise AI adoption
- Launched AI Fund (2018): $175M VC fund for AI startups
- Among first (2008) to advocate using GPUs for deep learning — now standard practice
- Board of Amazon (April 2024)
- Views on AI regulation: opposed California AI safety bill (SB 1047) as "massive liabilities for science-fiction risks"; strong advocate for open-source AI
- Strong advocate for democratizing AI education globally
- CS229 (Stanford ML course) is one of the most popular university courses ever; available free online
