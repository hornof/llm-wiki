## Bio

AI Engineering Leader. 1x founder (acquired), PhD in CS, former CTO and VP of Engineering. Currently returning to hands-on technical work in AI after building and scaling engineering organizations for 15+ years. This wiki is his compounding knowledge base for that transition.

Career arc: deep engineering and research roots (PhD CS, published at NeurIPS) → hands-on IC → engineering leadership at scale (Nervana Systems → Intel acquisition, scaled software team from 10 to 150 engineers across 4 countries) → founder (Luminide, AI model development tooling, acquired by Akridata) → now: hands-on AI Engineering.
