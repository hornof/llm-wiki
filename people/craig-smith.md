## Who They Are

Craig S. Smith is the host of **Eye on A.I.**, a weekly longform-interview podcast tracking developments in artificial intelligence research. Long-time *New York Times* journalist. The podcast format: extended one-on-one conversations with practitioners, researchers, and operators, putting incremental advances into broader context. First wiki capture via the 2026-05-15 [[eye-on-ai-lovejoy-agentic-enterprise-2026-05-15|Kris Lovejoy interview]].
