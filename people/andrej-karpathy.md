## Who They Are
AI researcher and educator. Co-founded OpenAI, led Tesla Autopilot's vision team, returned to OpenAI, then went independent. One of the most influential voices in practical AI/ML — known for making complex concepts accessible without dumbing them down. Strong following among both researchers and practitioners.

## Their Current Focus
Independent work on AI education and tooling. Published the LLM Wiki pattern for building personal knowledge bases using LLMs. Also known for neural network tutorials (nanoGPT, micrograd) and the "Software 2.0" framing.
