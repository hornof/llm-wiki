## Who They Are

Charlie Hills (@charliejhills) is an independent creator publishing on **charliehills.substack.com** about practical [[skill-md|Claude skills]] and plugin workflows. His output leans toward **legible, packaged, installable** Claude-skill collections and infographics that translate the skills/plugins ecosystem into consumer-friendly mental models — a distribution-and-packaging voice in the Claude-skills space rather than a frontier-research or vendor one.

## Their Current Focus

- **"Build Your Whole Team with Claude" (2026-07-12)** ([[charliejhills-claude-whole-company-42-skills-2026-07-12]]): a viral thread + infographic laying out **42 Claude skills as a real company org chart** (`claude-code` as "the operating system"/CEO over seven departments), every tile a real, installable skill or plugin from GitHub + `claude.com/plugins`. Emphasis on *"every skill is actually installable, not concept art."*

## Notable Takes

- **Skills-as-org-chart**: frames an operator's Claude setup as a company you "staff" with skills by department (Developers / Designers / Marketing / Social Media / Finance / Small Business / Legal) rather than by headcount — a consumer-legible packaging of the [[ai-native-organizations|AI-native-organization]] thesis.
- **Installability over concept-art**: the through-line is that the whole chart is real and reproducible, not aspirational — positioning himself against the "slop" end of AI-content.
