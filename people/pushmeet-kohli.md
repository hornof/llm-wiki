## Who They Are
Head of the AI for Science group at [[google-deepmind]]. Has led this group for nearly a decade — it was formally started the day after the AlphaGo Seoul match (~2016). The group works on applying DeepMind's AI capabilities to real scientific challenges: biology, drug discovery, weather simulation, and more.

## Their Current Focus
AI for science applications including the virtual cell project, weather simulation ([[google-deepmind]]'s WeatherNext), and the broader mission of using AI to accelerate scientific discovery in biology, materials science, and energy.
