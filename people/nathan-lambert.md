## Who They Are

Until ~mid-2026 he was at the **Allen Institute for AI (AI2)** working on open-source / open-weight model research; **departed AI2 in early June 2026** to *"continue open-source AI work"* independently (per [[dailybrief-roundup-2026-06-02-brief|Jun 02 Daily Brief]]).
