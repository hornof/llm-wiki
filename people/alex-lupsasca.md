## Who They Are
Theoretical physicist on OpenAI's Science team. Specializes in black hole physics and quantum gravity. Recipient of the 2024 Breakthrough Prize in Fundamental Physics (often called the "Oscar for physics"). Created the Black Hole Explorer iPhone app. Published influential papers on black hole photon rings. Connected to Harvard via collaborations with Andrew Strominger.

## Their Current Focus
- Frontier theoretical physics work using GPT-5.x as a research collaborator
- Coined term "vibe physics" by analogy with [[vibe-coding]]; using iterative dialogue with frontier models to extend the boundary of theoretical physics knowledge
- Documented production of novel gluon amplitude and graviton results using GPT-5.2

## Notable Takes
- **Vibe physics as frontier extension**: distinguishes the practice from [[ai-for-science]] applications like [[alphafold]] — the AI is generating *new theoretical insights*, not interpolating on a well-defined existing problem. — [[latent-space-lupsasca-vibe-physics-2026-05]]
- **Gluon amplitudes claim**: GPT-5.2 derived non-zero "single-minus gluon tree amplitudes" under specific conditions, contradicting prior assumptions — using a "half-collinear regime" limit. Lupsasca's team had been stuck on this problem >1 year; GPT solved it in one week. — [[latent-space-lupsasca-vibe-physics-2026-05]]
- **Graviton extension claim**: 110 pages of novel quantum gravity calculations extending the gluon work to gravitons; the model adapted techniques specifically for gravitons' unique mathematical properties rather than transposing the gluon approach. Three weeks of human verification followed. — [[latent-space-lupsasca-vibe-physics-2026-05]]
- **Reproduction speed**: "When GPT5 came out, it was able to reproduce one of my best papers... in 30 minutes." — [[latent-space-lupsasca-vibe-physics-2026-05]]
- **Frontier capability claim**: "At the science frontier, the capabilities were really taking off." — [[latent-space-lupsasca-vibe-physics-2026-05]]
- **Technique import**: "ChatGPT imported new techniques that were necessary... and used them flawlessly." — [[latent-space-lupsasca-vibe-physics-2026-05]]
