## Who They Are
CEO of Google DeepMind. Nobel laureate in Chemistry (2024, for AlphaFold). PhD in neuroscience from UCL; postdoc at MIT. Co-founded DeepMind in 2009 (acquired by Google), which produced AlphaGo, AlphaFold, and systems that won gold medals at the International Math Olympiad. Also runs [[isomorphic-labs]] as a spin-out. Takes a neuroscience-grounded approach to AI — studies the human brain as the only confirmed existence proof of general intelligence.

Background before DeepMind: chess prodigy; worked at Bullfrog Productions (Theme Park, ~10M+ copies sold, age ~17); founded Elixir Studios straight out of college (Republic — full country simulation; too ambitious for Pentium-era hardware, a formative lesson).

## Their Current Focus
Building toward AGI at Google DeepMind, with an explicit 2030 target. Simultaneously running [[isomorphic-labs]] on drug discovery. Publicly holds a stricter definition of AGI than most in the industry. Focused on the fundamental gaps between current systems and true general intelligence: continual learning, creativity, long-term planning. Describes the current moment as "the agent era."
